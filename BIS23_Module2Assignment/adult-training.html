Adult Training Program

Train like a fighter—whether you’re a beginner, returning, or leveling up your conditioning.

Training Options

  • Beginner Boxing: fundamentals, technique, and confidence building
  • Fitness Boxing: high-energy workouts (no sparring required)
  • Intermediate/Advanced: pads, bag work, footwork, and conditioning circuits
  • 1-on-1 Coaching: personalized training plans and skill focus

Suggested Schedule

Weekdays: 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM

Weekends: 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM
