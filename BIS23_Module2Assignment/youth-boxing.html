Youth Boxing Program

Build confidence, discipline, and fitness in a safe, structured environment.

What Kids Learn

  • Boxing fundamentals (stance, footwork, basic combinations)
  • Conditioning (endurance, strength, coordination)
  • Discipline & respect (focus, teamwork, sportsmanship)
  • Goal setting (progress tracking and skill development)

Ages & Schedule

Ages: 8–17 (beginner-friendly)

Suggested Schedule: Mon/Wed/Fri — 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM
